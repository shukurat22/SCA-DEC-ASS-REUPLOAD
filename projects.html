MY PROJECTS CATALOGUE

This section include the projects I collaborated with some product teams as a Product manager.

Product name: Recruitradar

Product manager (Volunteer)

RecruitRadar is a decentralized recruitment platform where employers post jobs available in their organizations and allow job seekers to search, find and apply for jobs that match their qualifications leveraging AI integration for Resume check & ranking and employee application analysis. It uses a B2B2C model that focuses on businesses/employers needing recruitment solutions and also ensures job seekers, as end-users, can seamlessly benefit from the opportunities provided by those businesses.

Product name: My Quiz Pal

Product manager Trainee & Project Team lead (Capstone Project)

MyQuizPal is a web-based quiz application that can be used to conduct assessments by teachers or tutors for their students in secondary schools and can also be used by students to practice questions based on their subject preferences as well as used in practising past questions to prepare ahead for external examinations such as WAEC, NECO, GCE and JAMB with an immediate grading and constructive correction feedback. It usesn B2B2C model, whereby the B2B aspect involves schools and tutors purchasing or subscribing to the platform for classroom and institutional use while the B2C aspect allows students to use the platform directly, either through school subscriptions or independent access, to enhance their learning and exam preparation.
